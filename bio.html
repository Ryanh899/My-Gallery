Bio.

I am a San Clemente born photographer, surfer, and waterman. I fell in love with photography when I bought my first film camera, a Ricoh Kr-30 sp program, at the local Salvation Army.

Since then my love and passion for photography has grown and I have refined my skills as a photographer. I love capturing memories and moments and will continute to do that for as long as I can. I spealize in Surf, Lifestyle, Underwater, and Portrait photography but am always looking to expand my horizons.
